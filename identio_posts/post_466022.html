SOLIDFILES cierra el 31 de mayo 2017

  • Categoría: Noticias
  • Publicado hace más de 8 años
Solidfiles confirmo hoy el cierre definitivo de su servicio a partir del 31/5/2017,si bien en la pagina no hay ningun anuncio,los que tenemos cuentas ahi,recibimos un mail de confirmacion y ademas al loguearse en la respectiva cuenta aparece arriba de todo un aviso


Good bye!
You are receiving this e-mail because you are an active user of Solidfiles.

After almost 10 years, the end has finally come. Due to declining revenues from advertisers, it's sadly no longer economically viable for us to keep running Solidfiles. Over the past year, we've tried to take measures to keep the site running, but it has unfortunately not been enough. On May 31, 2017 we'll be shutting down the servers for good.

Make sure to download any files that you want to keep before May 31, 2017!

For our Premium subscribers, we have cancelled all subscriptions and will be issuing partial refunds for any time remaining after May 31st.

It has been a great journey and we hope that you have enjoyed using Solidfiles for all these years.

Regards,
The Solidfiles Team

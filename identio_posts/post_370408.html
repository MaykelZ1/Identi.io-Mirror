When the setting: Settings > Saving and Startup > Landing Page == Off
Using thrown weapons while on foot (when it is the selected weapon) will then cause the game to crash if:
1) You enter a vehicle and aim.
2) There is a character transition (across the map).

You can use thrown weapons from inside a car or using quick throw. This won't cause a crash to occur.
SOLUTION

When the setting: Settings > Saving and Startup > Landing Page == On
After restarting the game you can then use throwables on foot to your hearts content.
Apparently this solves the crashes related to it.
